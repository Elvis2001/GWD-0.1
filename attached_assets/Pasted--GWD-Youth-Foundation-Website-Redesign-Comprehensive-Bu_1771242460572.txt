# GWD Youth Foundation Website Redesign - Comprehensive Build Prompt

## Project Overview
Redesign the **Grow With Data Youth Foundation (GWDYF)** website (gwd.com.ng) with modern, engaging design while maintaining the organization's mission, branding, and content integrity. The redesign should be visually stunning, highly interactive, and user-friendly.

---

## Organization Background

### About GWDYF
**Grow With Data Youth Foundation (GWDYF)** is a Nigerian non-profit organization dedicated to empowering young Nigerians through financial literacy, leadership development, and entrepreneurship training.

**Mission:** To provide exciting and unique opportunities for children and young adults' learning and community building, bringing together youths from diverse backgrounds under the common objective of learning financial and money skills while having fun.

**Founded by:** Nnenna Mosugu (Executive Director & Founder)

**Core Focus Areas:**
- Financial Literacy
- Leadership Development
- Entrepreneurship
- Youth Empowerment

**Target Audience:**
- Secondary school students
- Higher institution students (via HUBs)
- Unemployed youths
- Young Nigerians seeking financial knowledge

**Location:** Suite 2/1, 2nd Floor, G.I. Mega Plaza, #40/42 Lamido Ahmadu Road Off Lamido Zubairu Way, Opposite Federal Medical Center (FMC) Yola Bye-pass, Yola 640001, Nigeria

**Contact:** +234 8035867458

#### Financial Literacy Curriculum (Featured on Homepage)
Students will learn to:

1. **Understand the value of a college education**
   - Career planning and earnings potential
   - The importance of graduating

2. **Make and follow a personal budget**
   - Track personal income and expenses
   - Understanding budgeting rules of thumb

3. **Understand loans and credit**
   - Basic terms and disclosure rules
   - Responsible vs. Excessive borrowing
   - Loan amortization
   - Good and bad loans
   - Assets and Liabilities

4. **Understand the role of finances in the context of setting life goals**

5. **Make first steps into employment after graduation**
   - Self-employment
   - Entrepreneurship
   - Evaluating job offers
   - Student loan repayment

6. **Understand banking basics**
   - Checking and saving accounts
   - Debit cards
   - Interest and other banking terms

7. **Understand and purchase insurance**
   - Auto, renters, health, home, and life insurance

8. **Know how to plan for the future**
   - Establishing an emergency fund
   - Plan for major expenditures
   - Rationally make big financial decisions
   - Investment terms and instruments, including retirement

9. **Make plans for charitable giving**

10. **Take personal responsibility** and have a sense of personal agency for one's financial situation

## Design Requirements

### Visual Design Principles

1. **Modern & Clean Aesthetic**
   - Spacious layouts with ample whitespace
   - Card-based design for content sections
   - Smooth rounded corners on elements
   - Subtle shadows for depth

2. **Animation & Interactivity**
   - **Ken Burns effect** on hero images and gallery photos
   - **Hover effects** on all interactive elements:
     - Cards that lift/scale on hover
     - Button transformations
     - Image overlays with information reveal
   - **Framer Motion animations:**
     - Scroll-triggered animations
     - Staggered entrance animations for content blocks
     - Smooth page transitions
     - Parallax effects on hero sections
   - Smooth scroll behavior throughout

3. **Color Theme Implementation**
   - **Light Mode:** Clean, bright, with primary green accents
   - **Dark Mode:** Deep backgrounds with vibrant color accents
   - **Theme Toggle:** Smooth transition between themes
   - Position toggle in top-right of navigation bar
   - Animated sun/moon icon for theme switcher

4. **Typography Hierarchy**
   - **Hero Headlines:** Extra large, bold, attention-grabbing (60-80px)
   - **Section Headers:** Large, bold (36-48px)
   - **Subheadings:** Medium bold (24-32px)
   - **Body Text:** Comfortable reading size (16-18px)
   - **Use color overlays** on text over images for readability

5. **Responsive Design**
   - Mobile-first approach
   - Breakpoints: Mobile (< 768px), Tablet (768px-1024px), Desktop (> 1024px)
   - Touch-friendly interface elements on mobile
   - Hamburger menu for mobile navigation

## Animation Details

### Ken Burns Effect
- Apply to:
  - Hero section backgrounds
  - Gallery images
  - Blog post featured images
  - Testimonial backgrounds
- Subtle zoom (1.0 to 1.1 scale) over 10-15 seconds
- Infinite loop or pause on hover

### Hover Effects
- **Cards:** Scale(1.05) + shadow increase
- **Buttons:** Background color shift + slight scale
- **Images:** Zoom + overlay opacity change
- **Links:** Color change + underline animation
- **Navigation items:** Underline slide-in or color fade

### Framer Motion Animations
- **Scroll Animations:**
  - Fade in from bottom (stagger for lists)
  - Slide in from left/right
  - Scale up from 0.8 to 1
  
- **Page Transitions:**
  - Fade in/out
  - Slide transitions
  
- **Stagger Effects:**
  - Blog post cards
  - Gallery items
  - Curriculum items
  - Team members
  
- **Parallax:**
  - Background images scroll slower than content
  - Hero section elements at different speeds

### Loading States
- Skeleton screens for content loading
- Spinner or progress indicators
- Smooth content reveal
